Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

I offer full-cycle game development services including concept development, prototyping, programming, UI/UX design, testing, and deployment. I specialize in creating WebGL games and interactive experiences.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. Small projects may take 1-2 months, while more complex games can take 3-6 months or more. I'll provide a detailed timeline estimate after our initial consultation.

Do you work with remote clients?

Yes, I work with clients globally. Communication is maintained through regular video calls, emails, and project management tools to ensure smooth collaboration regardless of location.

How do you handle game hosting and distribution?

I can help with setting up your game on various platforms including itch.io, Steam, mobile app stores, or your own website with WebGL implementation. This includes the technical setup and optimization for each platform.
